Discription Box

coffee beans
Roasted seeds from the coffee plant, ground and brewed to make coffee.
They contain caffeine, giving coffee its rich flavor and stimulating effect.
cremear
A dairy or non-dairy additive used to make coffee smoother and creamier.
It softens the bitterness and adds a rich texture.
suger
A sweetener added to coffee to reduce bitterness and enhance flavor balance.
Common types are white sugar, brown sugar, or syrup.
